University Advice: Gabriela Lagos

Emory (2021)

My college experience was nothing short of amazing! I made tons of new friends from all different backgrounds and learned so much about myself in the process. One piece of advice I have for college is to push yourself out of your comfort zone. Join clubs, attend events, and say "yes" to new experiences. You never know who you'll meet or what you'll learn. Another tip is to prioritize your mental health. College can be stressful, but it's important to take time for yourself and practice self-care. Whether that means taking a break from studying to hit the gym or calling a friend to vent, it's crucial to prioritize your well-being. Oh, and don't forget to take advantage of all the resources your school has to offer, from tutoring services to career advice. And when it comes to academics, don't be afraid to ask for help. Your professors are there to support you and want to see you succeed.
